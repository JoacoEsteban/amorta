# Blog Post Content Specification (AdSense-Oriented)

## 1. Objective

Produce high-quality, original, and useful articles that meet AdSense content quality thresholds and demonstrate clear value to users.

---

## 2. Content Requirements

### 2.1 Volume

- Minimum total posts before application: **15–30**
- Each post must be complete and independently valuable

### 2.2 Length

- Target: **800–1500+ words per article**
- Avoid thin content (<500 words)

### 2.3 Originality

- 100% original writing
- No scraping, spinning, or paraphrasing existing content
- No duplicate or near-duplicate pages

### 2.4 Topical Consistency

- Maintain a **single niche or tightly related topics**
- Avoid unrelated or random subjects

---

## 3. Article Structure

### 3.1 Title

- Clear, specific, and descriptive
- Avoid vague or clickbait phrasing

### 3.2 Headings

- Use logical hierarchy:
  - `# H1` (title)
  - `## H2` (main sections)
  - `### H3` (subsections)
- Ensure scannability and structure

### 3.3 Introduction

- Define the problem or topic immediately
- State what the reader will gain

### 3.4 Body Content

- Provide:
  - Explanations
  - Step-by-step guidance (if applicable)
  - Examples or use cases
- Avoid filler or repetition

### 3.5 Formatting

- Short paragraphs (2–4 lines)
- Use lists where appropriate
- Maintain consistent spacing

### 3.6 Conclusion

- Summarize key points
- Reinforce usefulness

---

## 4. Quality Criteria

### 4.1 Depth

- Must provide **non-trivial insight or actionable information**
- Content should not be easily replaceable by a search snippet

### 4.2 Clarity

- Direct, readable language
- No unnecessary jargon unless explained

### 4.3 Accuracy

- Factually correct and up to date
- No misleading or exaggerated claims

---

## 5. Internal Linking

- Link to **relevant articles within the site**
- Use contextual anchor text
- Minimum: 2–5 internal links per post (where applicable)

---

## 6. External Links

- Only link to **reliable, authoritative sources**
- Avoid spammy or irrelevant outbound links

---

## 7. SEO and Metadata

### 7.1 Basic Requirements

- Unique title per page
- Meta description (concise summary)
- Proper URL slug (short and descriptive)

### 7.2 Avoid

- Keyword stuffing
- Over-optimization

---

## 8. Media Usage

- Images optional but must be:
  - Relevant
  - Non-copyrighted or properly licensed
- No excessive or decorative-only media

---

## 9. Prohibited Content

Do NOT include:

- Copyright-infringing material
- Scraped or auto-generated pages
- Adult or explicit content
- Hate, violence, or harmful material
- Misleading, deceptive, or “made-for-ads” content

---

## 10. Readiness Criteria (Per Article)

Each article must:

- Be fully written and published (no drafts or placeholders)
- Contain no broken links
- Have proper formatting and structure
- Provide clear user value

---

## 11. Site-Level Consistency

- Uniform writing style across posts
- Consistent formatting and layout
- Logical categorization and navigation

---

## 12. Acceptance Threshold

A site is considered ready for AdSense when:

- ≥15–30 compliant articles are published
- All articles meet length, quality, and structure requirements
- Content demonstrates clear expertise or usefulness
- No policy violations are present
