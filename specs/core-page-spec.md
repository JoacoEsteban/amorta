# Core Pages Specification (AdSense Compliance)

## 1. Overview

Define requirements for four mandatory standalone pages:

- Privacy Policy
- About
- Contact
- Terms & Conditions

Each page must exist as a **dedicated URL**, fully populated, and accessible via global navigation.

---

## 2. Global Requirements (Applies to All Pages)

### 2.1 Accessibility

- Linked in **footer (mandatory)**
- Publicly accessible (no login required)
- No broken links

### 2.2 Completeness

- No placeholders or “coming soon”
- No template text left unedited
- Must reflect actual site behavior

### 2.3 Consistency

- Same branding and layout as main site
- Mobile responsive
- Readable formatting

---

## 3. Privacy Policy Specification

### 3.1 URL

`/privacy-policy`

### 3.2 Required Sections

#### 3.2.1 Introduction

- Identify the website
- State purpose of policy

#### 3.2.2 Data Collection

- Types of data collected:
  - Personal (e.g., email)
  - Non-personal (e.g., browser, IP)

#### 3.2.3 Data Usage

- Explain how data is used:
  - Analytics
  - Communication
  - Site improvement

#### 3.2.4 Cookies

- State use of cookies
- Explain purpose (tracking, preferences)

#### 3.2.5 Third-Party Services

- Explicitly mention:
  - Google AdSense
- State that third parties may use cookies for personalized ads

#### 3.2.6 User Rights

- Ability to disable cookies (browser settings)

#### 3.2.7 Contact Information

- Method to reach site owner regarding privacy concerns

---

## 4. About Page Specification

### 4.1 URL

`/about`

### 4.2 Required Sections

#### 4.2.1 Site Identity

- Who runs the site (individual, pseudonym, or company)

#### 4.2.2 Purpose

- Clear explanation of what the site provides

#### 4.2.3 Audience

- Who the content is for

#### 4.2.4 Credibility Signals

- Relevant experience, knowledge, or intent
- No requirement for formal credentials, but must appear legitimate

#### 4.2.5 Transparency

- Statement clarifying editorial intent (informational, educational, etc.)

---

## 5. Contact Page Specification

### 5.1 URL

`/contact`

### 5.2 Required Elements

#### 5.2.1 Contact Method

At least one:

- Functional contact form
- Visible email address

#### 5.2.2 Validation

- Form must submit successfully
- Email must be deliverable

#### 5.2.3 Optional عناصر

- Response expectations (optional)
- Basic categories (e.g., support, inquiries)

---

## 6. Terms & Conditions Specification

### 6.1 URL

`/terms` or `/terms-and-conditions`

### 6.2 Required Sections

#### 6.2.1 Acceptance of Terms

- Use of site implies agreement

#### 6.2.2 Use of Content

- Define permitted use (personal, non-commercial, etc.)

#### 6.2.3 Intellectual Property

- State ownership of site content

#### 6.2.4 Limitation of Liability

- Disclaimer for errors, outages, or misuse

#### 6.2.5 External Links

- No responsibility for third-party sites

#### 6.2.6 Modifications

- Right to update terms at any time

---

## 7. Navigation Requirements

### 7.1 Footer (Mandatory)

Must include links to:

- Privacy Policy
- About
- Contact
- Terms

### 7.2 URL Structure

- Clean, readable slugs
- No query strings or dynamic-only URLs

---

## 8. Acceptance Criteria

A site meets AdSense baseline compliance when:

- All four pages exist and are accessible
- Each page contains required sections
- No placeholders or incomplete content
- Privacy Policy explicitly references Google AdSense
- Contact method is functional
- Pages are linked in footer navigation
